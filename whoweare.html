Healthcare professionals are faced with an ever changing and growing compliance, regulations, and guidelines, which can be both challenging and time-consuming.

As new regulations and guidelines are implemented on a day in and day out basis, Healthcare professionals are left in a challenging yet highly confusing spiral of reforms and regulations without guidelines or education of when and how to keep up with this evolving healthcare system. From charts to EHR and prescription to Electronic Prescription; from HIPAA to Meaningful Use and PQRS to MECRA, our healthcare system is in the midst of a highly challenging and difficult to follow and implement regulations which in turn results in inefficiencies in effectively running and maintaining a healthcare business.

Every time a new reform or regulation is passed, physicians are faced with a myriad of questions of how and who can help them in educating and implementing these guidelines effectively, lack of training ability and this huge gap in the market directly reflects in driving the cost up due to lack of knowledge and unawareness of how and what to implement to comply with these reforms.

MedGinix is a physician-advocate based healthcare Contract Management Company that understands the minute intricacies of effectively and efficiently running a healthcare practice/business. MedGinix through its years of expertise and deep involvement in multiple IPAs, ACO, MSOs, and MSAs has developed a unique skillset of managing and delivering cost effective, compliance-based healthcare services and products.

From EHR to Meaningful use and electronic prescriptions to PQRS, we have done it all. MedGinix has successfully and cost effectively delivered healthcare solutions and services to thousands of Healthcare providers across the country. Our expertise in closely working with both payers and providers has given us a unique ability to manage healthcare contracts and services with highly competitive pricing structure, with Zero Tolerance in compliance errors.
